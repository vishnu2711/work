OBjectives:

The main objective of the Students Innovation Club (SIC) at VEMU Institute of Technology is to encourage, inspire and nurture young students by supporting them to work with new ideas and transform them into prototypes.

Outcomes:

To create a vibrant local innovation ecosystem.
Start-up supporting Mechanism in HEIs.
Prepare institute for Atal Ranking of Institutions on Innovation Achievements Framework.
Establish Function Ecosystem for Scouting Ideas and Pre-incubation of Ideas.
Develop better Cognitive Ability for Technology Students.

Work anywhere

We have freedom to work with anyone irrespective of department & Gender.

Collaboration

What SIC Provides ?

Students Innovation club is student startup platform.
Students Innovation club is empowered by Vemu Institute Of Technology, in collaboration with AIC-SKU Confederation is an Atal Incubation Centre (AIC) at Sri Krishnadevaraya University (SKU) supported by Atal Innovation Mission, NITI Aayog, Govt. of India and AP Innovation Society, Govt. of Andhra Pradesh. The Students Innovation club is students network and students platform where they can showcase there talents.
